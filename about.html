ABOUT THE ARTIST



HI! MY NAME IS ROYCE NAVORA I AM AN ILLUSTRATOR AND I AM CURRENTLY STUDYING BACHELOR OF MULTIMEDIA ARTS AT LYCEUM OF THE PHILIPPINES UNIVERSITY. I LIKE TO DRAW FAN ARTS FROM MY FAVORITE ANIMES, SHOWS, MANGA, AND GAMES. I DRAW DIGITALLY, I USE ADOBE PHOTOSHOP TO CREATE MY ARTS I LIKE TO DRAW THINGS, CHARACTERS THAT I AM A FAN OF, FOR NOW I MOSTLY FOCUS ON FAN ARTS BECAUSE I AM STILL EXPLORING ON DRAWING DIGITALLY SINCE I JUST STARTED TO DRAW DIGITALLY SINCE 2020. I MOSTLY DRAW TRADITIONALLY BUT I STARTED MY JOURNEY ON DRAWING DIGITALLY ON DECEMBER OF THE YEAR 2020. TRANSITIONING FROM TRADITIONAL TO DIGITAL IS HARD LIKE BEGINNERS MOSTLY DO, THE ILLUSTRATIONS THAT I DID ARE NOT SO GOOD BUT I STARTED RESEARCHING HOW TO DRAW DIGITALLY I RESEARCH THE METHODS ON DRAWING DIGITALLY AND I STARTED PRACTICING AND MADE ATLEAST ONE ILLUSTRATION A DAY THE RESULTS ARE WHAT MY PORTFOLIO IS NOW, I IMPROVED A LOT BY PRACTICING AND DRAWING ATLEAST ONE ILLUSTRATION A DAY AND ALSO REASEARCHING PROPER METHODS IN MAKING DIGITAL ILLUSTRATIONS. FOR NOW ALL MY ILLUSTRATIONS ARE FAN ARTS OF THE SHOWS, AND GAMES I LOVE, BUT IN THE FUTURE I WOULD LIKE TO CREATE MY OWN CONCEPT AND OWN STYLE OF ART AND I ALSO LIKE TO EXPLORE MORE WAYS TO CREATE DIGITAL ART AND I WOULD ALSO LIKE TO EXPLORE ANIMATION BUT FOR THE MEAN TIME I WILL FOCUS ON IMPROVING MY ILLUSTRATION.
